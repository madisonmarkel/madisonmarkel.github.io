Madison Markel

Hello! I'm Maddie, the person behind the screen. Meaning that not only is the artwork mine, so is the code! I was born and raised in Denver, Colorado with the privilege of not only experiencing city life, but the opportunity to escape to the mountains. That said, my love of nature bloomed in college and thereafter, influencing my art.

I am currently based in Seattle, Washington where I still combine and balance the city and mountain life.

I do my best to express everything I love. That includes nature (particularly hikes with lakes and good views), design, beer, and painting!
